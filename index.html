About NTF

Assessment for learning, not of learning

Founded in 2018 by a group of scientists, NERD Tools Foundation develops educational neuroscience research-based diagnostic and intervention tools. Our work is rooted in the belief that learning is individual — shaped by cognitive abilities, neuroplasticity, and environment.


We partner with government and private schools across India and Africa to identify children at risk for specific learning disabilities, and equip teachers and special educators to help every child reach their full potential.

Our mission

Develop and promote EdNeuro Research-based diagnostic and intervention tools that act as assistive technological aids for children with learning differences.

Our approach

We use cognitive development frameworks and validated tools in Indian languages to assess literacy, numeracy, and social-emotional learning — in classrooms and in the field.

Research focus

Areas of active inquiry

Cognitive development

Studying how children acquire cognitive skills and what markers indicate risk for learning difficulties early on.

Literacy & numeracy

Designing validated assessments for reading, writing, and mathematical understanding across diverse linguistic contexts.

Personalised remediation

Building adaptive learning tools that tailor interventions to each child's unique cognitive profile.

Social & emotional learning

Currently developing SEL assessment tools to support children's emotional wellbeing alongside academic growth.

Immersive learning

Designing new paradigms for interactive and immersive learning, assessment, and remediation using advances in technology.

Multilingual tools

Web-based cognitive assessments available in multiple Indian languages, validated for cultural and linguistic contexts.
